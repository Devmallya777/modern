The Genesis of Gifting

ZARIA was not built to follow trends; it was engineered to encapsulate time. We viewed the traditional concept of gifting and stripped it down to its most raw, emotional core. Every suite is a physical manifestation of high-end horology mixed with the finest textile craftsmanship.

02

Uncompromising Craft

From hand-dyed mulberry silk to laser-etched obsidian plating, our materials are sourced from the absolute pinnacle of their respective industries. There is no mass production here. Each ribbon is tied by hand, and every wax seal is poured with deliberate, uncompromising precision.

03

The Architect

Under the creative direction of Eshna Singha Chakraborty and Devmallya Chakraborty, the ZARIA atelier bridges the gap between mechanical perfection and soft, artistic elegance. We do not just build boxes; we construct highly secure, beautiful vaults for your most treasured human connections.
